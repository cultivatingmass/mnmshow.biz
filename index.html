About me

Hi, my name is Maria. I am a passionate Visual Desiner based in Los Angeles. I like to use my computer to create websites, print designs, and motion graphics. I also enjoy editing photographs and videos.

Design became a thrill for me when I finally discovered it was my passion at 19 years old. Since then, I've been aiming to expand my skill sets and strengthen my creative muscles everyday.

What I Love to Do

Web Design

Before I start coding any website, I start out with pen and paper. I figure the information hierarchy of my layout based on importance. I do this by dividing sections of content and then I figure out where and how to display each section in the webpage. I then continue with interactivity, colors, and typography.

Motion Graphics

I work on Adobe After Effects for text and image animation, rotoscoping, motion tracking, and visual fx. I start out with a vision in my mind, and then I persist with it until I get my piece to move just right.

Photography

I like to incorporate my own photographs into my projects whenever possible. I use a digital SLR for my professional photographs. I have shot and edited product photographs for my clients before, and I am happy to do so again.

Video Production

I am trained in narrative video production. I am capable of conceptualizing and managing a project in all its stages: pre-production (storyboards and scripts), production (video shooting and audio recording, lighting), to post-production (editing and creating graphics and effects).
